B2B Analytics

ЦУМ STAT

Mobile analytics dashboard for boutique managers at ЦУМ. Bringing complex sales data to phone in a clear, scannable format.

Role Product Designer
Platform Mobile App
Segment B2B · Retail Analytics
Year 2023
Hero / cover image

Overview

Boutique managers at ЦУМ needed to monitor sales, revenue, and staff performance on the go. The existing reporting tools were desktop-only and required manual exports — making daily decision-making slow and fragmented.

The core challenge: display large volumes of analytics data on a phone screen without overwhelming the user, while keeping key metrics immediately visible.

Context / problem screens

Research & Methodology

I conducted structured interviews with 6 boutique managers to map out their daily reporting workflows and identify the most critical data points they needed at a glance.

Key needs identified:

  • Sales dynamics and revenue by category — checked every morning
  • Renters report with brand-level and seasonal performance
  • Mark-up reports showing margin analysis by brand and category
  • CRM section: seller effectiveness and customer behaviour metrics
Manager workflow map
Data hierarchy analysis

Design Challenges & Solutions

Challenge 1 — Data density on mobile

Analytics dashboards are typically built for wide desktop screens. I solved this by grouping related metrics into categorical blocks, using simplified tables with highlighted key values, and designing growth and deviation indicators that communicate trend direction instantly.

Main statistics screen

Challenge 2 — Renters & Mark-Up reports

Managers needed to compare performance across multiple brands and time periods. I designed a tabbed report layout with filterable brand cards and a clear seasonal comparison view that fits comfortably within a single scroll.

Renters report
Mark-up report

Challenge 3 — CRM Section

The CRM view needed to show seller effectiveness and customer behaviour without feeling like a spreadsheet. I used ranked lists with inline progress indicators and colour-coded performance tiers to make comparisons immediate.

CRM section screens

Key Results

The dashboard received positive feedback from all stakeholder groups during internal testing and was approved for production rollout.
