VIP-Platinum

Redesign of the loyalty program and checkout flow for a luxury boutique. Improved conversion, reduced bounce, and launched a chat-to-purchase feature.

Role Product Designer
Platform Mobile App
Segment B2C · Luxury Retail
Year 2023
Hero / cover image

Overview

VIP-Platinum is a loyalty program for VIP clients of a luxury boutique (ЦУМ). The existing mobile app had poor conversion on product pages and no direct communication channel between clients and personal stylists.

My goal was to redesign the product discovery and checkout experience, add alternative product matching, and introduce an in-app chat with consultants.

Before state / context screens

Research & Methodology

I used the Jobs-to-be-Done framework to conduct customer interviews with VIP clients. The core insight: users didn't come to browse — they came to solve a specific style need, and they wanted expert guidance, not an overwhelming product catalogue.

Key findings from 8 in-depth interviews:

  • Users abandoned product pages when nothing matched their initial search
  • They expected a "personal shopper" experience, not a generic e-commerce flow
  • High willingness to chat with a consultant before making a luxury purchase

Design Challenges & Solutions

Challenge 1 — Alternative product matching

When a product is out of stock or doesn't match, users need a path forward. I designed a swipe-based alternative matching interface that shows curated similar items from the same brand tier — keeping users in the purchase flow instead of dropping off.

Alternative matching UI screens

Challenge 2 — In-app chat with consultant

Users wanted expert advice before committing to a luxury purchase. I designed a product-aware chat interface where the consultant can see exactly what the client is looking at and push curated alternatives directly into the conversation.

Chat UI — client view
Chat UI — consultant view

Challenge 3 — Curated collections

Personal stylists needed a way to create and share curated collections with their clients. I designed a collection builder inside the consultant CMS and a clean, editorial presentation layer on the client side.

Curated collections UI

Key Results

Measured over 3 months post-launch against the previous app version.

+37% Conversion Rate
−22% Product Page Bounce
+15% Average Order Value
70% Chat→Checkout Rate

Design Deliverables

  • Full UI kit for the VIP client app
  • Product-aware chat interface (client + consultant views)
  • Alternative product matching component
  • Stylist collection builder and client collection view
  • Annotated handoff specs for development
